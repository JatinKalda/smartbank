About HSBC

HSBC Holdings plc is a British multinational universal bank and financial services company. Headquartered in London, HSBC is the world's third-largest bank by total assets and serves millions of customers worldwide.

With over 150 years of history, HSBC has built a reputation for reliability, innovation, and customer service excellence. We are committed to providing financial solutions that help our customers achieve their goals.
